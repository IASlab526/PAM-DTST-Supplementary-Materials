## Appendix

The appendix file contains the experimental conditions based on the Taguchi L9 orthogonal array. The table lists 108 experimental trials, including the actuation configuration, excitation frequency, tendon pretension, and PAM pressure.

File: [`appendix/APPENDIX.pdf`](appendix/APPENDIX.pdf)

## Actuation Configurations

- **NO PAM**: baseline configuration without a pneumatic artificial muscle.
- **PEA**: parallel elastic actuation configuration.
- **SEA**: series elastic actuation configuration.
- **SPEA**: series–parallel elastic actuation configuration.

## Video Information

The uploaded video shows representative experimental motion under the following conditions displayed in the video frame:

- NO PAM: `f = 0.75 Hz`, `T_pre = 45 N`
- SEA: `f = 0.75 Hz`, `T_pre = 45 N`, `P = 13 psi`
- PEA: `f = 0.75 Hz`, `T_pre = 45 N`, `P = 5 psi`
- SPEA: `f = 0.75 Hz`, `T_pre = 45 N`, `P = 5 psi`
